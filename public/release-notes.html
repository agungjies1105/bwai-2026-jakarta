Initial Gastronomic Release

May 23, 2026

Welcome to LuxeBite, a premium gourmet food ordering application crafted for the finest culinary experiences.

Features

  • Premium luxury UI/UX with dark theme, gold accents, and fluid layouts.
  • Interactive gastronomic menu showcasing Burgers, Pizzas, Elixirs, and Decadent Desserts.
  • Live Kitchen & Logistics Console for real-time tracking of gourmet orders.
  • Secure checkout mock-system with strict client and server-side validation.
  • Hardened state-bound session management and anti-CSRF token verification.

  • Enhanced server response times for order generation and state transitions.
